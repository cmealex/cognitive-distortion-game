About Cognitive Distortion Matcher

This game helps you learn to identify common cognitive distortions - the negative thought patterns that can contribute to anxiety and depression. By matching situations with the appropriate cognitive distortion type, you can become more aware of these patterns in your own thinking.

Cognitive distortions are patterns of thinking that are often inaccurate and negatively biased. They can worsen emotional difficulties and lead to poor decision-making. By learning to identify these patterns, you can begin to challenge and reframe these thoughts, leading to improved mental well-being.

This game challenges you to match real-life situations with the type of cognitive distortion they represent. As you progress through the levels, the matches become more challenging, helping you develop a deeper understanding of these thought patterns.

Practice regularly to improve your skills at identifying these patterns in daily life, which is the first step toward addressing them.
